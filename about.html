WHO WE ARE

Dabuči SRO is a total agricultural value chain solutions provider ranging from agricultural production, processing, domestic, import and export marketing. It’s both a Czech and Nigerian licensed company and is currently focused on the export of Sesame seeds, dried Chili Pepper, Dried Ginger, Cashew Nuts, Cocoa butter, Cocoa cake, Melon, Bitter kola, Plantain and Plantain chips, Soya beans, Kidney beans, Sweet potatoes, Maize and Charcoal We are located in Prague and then at the heart of the Nigeria’s agricultural zone, and have direct access to cash commodity markets, where we have on-location warehouses, and buying centers. We operate through a network of buying agents and commodity brokers, but also sometimes procure directly from the farmers. We ensure that as a matter of corporate policy the bulk of our produce are sourced from small farmers. The Company is led by experienced professionals with significant experience in Nigeria’s agribusiness space. The Company’s key operating executives have excellent track record of exporting agricultural commodities to the United States, Europe and Asia. …
